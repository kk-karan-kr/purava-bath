

# Purava — Premium Brass Health Faucets Website

A sleek, business-oriented website showcasing Purava's premium brass health faucets, hooks, and shower tubes with an emphasis on craftsmanship, elegance, and professionalism.

---

## Design System
- **Color palette**: Rich brass/gold (#B8860B, #D4A853), chrome silver (#C0C0C0), clean whites, subtle navy blue (#1B2A4A) accents
- **Typography**: Modern serif headings (Playfair Display style via Google Fonts) paired with clean sans-serif body text (Inter)
- **Layout**: Generous white space, large imagery, smooth scroll animations

---

## Homepage Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-width, immersive hero with a gradient overlay on a brass-toned background
- Tagline: **"Refresh. Rinse. Reimagine Clean."**
- CTA button: **"Explore Our Collection"**
- Subtle animated shimmer effect on the brass accent elements

### 2. About Us
- Split layout: brand story on one side, craftsmanship imagery on the other
- Highlights: Made in India, premium brass materials, decades of expertise
- Key stats displayed (e.g., years of experience, products delivered, happy clients)

### 3. Product Showcase
- Elegant card grid featuring product models: **Queen, Ruby, Cosmo** (and others)
- Each card shows product image placeholder, name, and key features (low maintenance, hot/cold water compatible, durable brass)
- Hover effects with subtle brass-toned accent animations

### 4. Features / Why Purava
- Icon-driven section highlighting: Durability, Ease of Use, Design Aesthetics, Premium Materials
- Clean visual layout with brass-colored icons

### 5. Business Partners
- Dedicated section for contractors, interior designers, and commercial buyers
- Value propositions: bulk pricing, consistent quality, upscale design compatibility
- CTA to request a partnership inquiry

### 6. Contact / Inquiry Form
- Clean form with fields: Name, Email, Phone, Inquiry Type (Business Inquiry / Product Samples / General), Message
- Brass-accented submit button

### 7. Footer
- Contact info, social media links, trust symbols/certifications
- "Made in India with Pride" badge

---

## Pages
- **Single-page scrolling homepage** with smooth navigation anchor links
- Sticky top navigation bar with Purava logo, section links, and a "Contact Us" CTA button
